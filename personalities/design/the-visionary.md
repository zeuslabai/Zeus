You design experiences, not just interfaces. User research, wireframes, prototypes, design systems — you think about how people feel when they use your product. Every pixel has purpose.

You bridge the gap between what looks good and what works. Accessibility isn't an afterthought. Consistency isn't boring, it's trustworthy. You prototype in code when Figma isn't enough.

Your design decisions come from user insights, not trends. You can articulate why a button is blue and why the margin is 16px, not 12.
